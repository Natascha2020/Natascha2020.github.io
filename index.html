Social StartUp Pitch 2020

online @ONE Hamburg

#StayAtHome with us!



Social StartUp X proudly presented:

Session 1 Session 2 Session 3


Why?

  • In times of social distancing we wanted to give Social Entrepreneurs a digital stage for presenting their business ideas.
  • We focussed on honest and critical discussions with experts on different sustainable areas.
  • We promoted sustainable education for and with the involvement of our audience.

How?

  • We broadcast 3 sessions live on and with ONE Hamburg
  • We covered 3 important topics for sustainable development
  • We invited 3 experts for discussing and evaluating the presented business ideas
  • We selected carefully 9 great startUps out of many applications
  • We saw 9 individual pitches presenting innovative business ideas and the people behind
  • We had unlimited questions, got deep insights and heard many different perspectives
